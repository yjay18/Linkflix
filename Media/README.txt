Drop your movies and shows here, then open Linkflix → Settings → "Scan media folders".

Linkflix reads the file/folder names, figures out what each title is (matching
TVMaze for shows and Wikipedia for films), and adds them to your library
automatically — asking you to confirm anything it can't identify.

Tips for best auto-matching (it's forgiving, but this helps):
  Movies:  Movie Name (2024).mkv        or  Movie.Name.2024.1080p.mkv
  Shows:   Show Name/Season 01/Show.Name.S01E02.mkv
           Show Name - 1x02 - Episode Title.mkv

Any video type works (MKV, MP4, …). You can also add folders from anywhere on
your Mac in Settings instead of putting files here.
