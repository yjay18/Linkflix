Drop your exported files in this folder:

  library.json — your titles, links and covers (share this one)
  watch.json   — your personal watch history / continue-watching (optional, private)

The app loads library.json automatically on first run, and Settings →
"Reload from folder" pulls in the latest versions of both anytime.
Share this folder (e.g. via Google Drive) so friends with the app get
your library too.
